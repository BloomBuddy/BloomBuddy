## 🌱 What is BloomBuddy?

**BloomBuddy** is a holistic, AI-driven therapeutic companion app for autistic children. It combines gamified learning, real-time emotion monitoring, and intelligent reporting — creating a safe, adaptive, and engaging environment where every child can grow at their own pace.

The platform is designed with three core stakeholders in mind: **the child**, **the parent**, and **the treating doctor** — keeping everyone connected and informed throughout every session.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 🎮 Therapeutic Game Suite

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🧩 **Skill Development Games** | Interactive games designed to build cognitive, social, and functional life skills |
| 🧠 **Memory Games** | Engaging pattern and recall challenges to strengthen working memory |
| 🥦 **Nutrition Adventure** | Fun gameplay that teaches children the importance of healthy eating habits |
| 🤲 **Motor Skill Games** | Activities tailored to improve fine and gross motor coordination |

---

### 🎨 Adaptive Canvas

A unique creative feature where the **caregiver draws something**, and the **child replicates it** — building hand-eye coordination, focus, and creative expression. The AI monitors the child's attempts and adapts difficulty progressively.

```
Caregiver draws  →  Child replicates  →  AI evaluates & adapts  →  Skills improve
```

---

### 🤖 Virtual Companion & Chatbot

BloomBuddy features a **friendly virtual character** that interacts with the child in real time:

- 💬 Conversational AI chatbot for guided interaction
- 👁️ **Eye-tracking integration** to monitor engagement and gaze patterns
- Helps children practice **conversation skills** and social responsiveness
- Adapts tone, pace, and complexity based on the child's comfort level

---

### 💚 Real-Time Emotion Monitoring (AI-Backed)

At the heart of BloomBuddy is continuous, AI-driven **emotion analysis** throughout every session:

- Monitors the child's emotional state in real time
- Ensures the child remains **calm, comfortable, and safe**
- Detects subtle shifts in emotion that may indicate distress
- Acts as a protective layer throughout all activities

---

## 🚨 Safety & Alert System

BloomBuddy takes child safety seriously with an **automated alert infrastructure**:

```
Session Starts
    └──► 📧 Notification sent to Parent & Doctor

During Session
    └──► 🔍 Emotion AI continuously monitors child

Sudden Emotional Change Detected
    └──► 🚨 Emergency Signal instantly sent to Parent & Doctor
```

No delay. No manual steps. Immediate response when it matters most.

---

## 📊 Consolidated Session Reports

At the end of every session, a **detailed report is automatically generated and emailed** to both the parent and the treating doctor. Reports include:

- 📈 Emotional journey throughout the session
- 🎯 Games played and performance metrics
- 🎨 Adaptive Canvas progress
- 💬 Interaction quality from the virtual companion
- 📌 Highlights and areas needing attention

> *Keeping caregivers and clinicians fully informed — every session, every time.*
